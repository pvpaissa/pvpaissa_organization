Chaos Cup III

Overview

The Feast Chaos Cup III is a community-held FINAL FANTASY XIV PvP tournament held in the Chaos Datacenter to pit the best European teams against one another following the end of the Feast Regional Championship 2018. The tournament will be fought exclusively online in The Feast (Custom Matches â€“ Crystal Tower).

All matches from the quarterfinals onwards will be streamed live on Twitch.

Team Composition & Eligibility

Teams can have a maximum number of 6 players, and must be able to respect the usual Feast team composition.

All FINAL FANTASY XIV Chaos players are allowed to join one participating team.

Organization

The Feast Chaos Cup III will consist in a Single-Elimination Tournament where the losing team of each match is immediately eliminated from the tournament.

All matches prior to the finals will be a series of best-of-three (BO3) rounds.

The finals will be a best-of-five (BO5) match.

Provided there are more than 8 registered teams, a preliminary phase will take place to determined the teams that enter quarterfinals. More information will be given later to participating teams.

Rewards

An optional Paypal Donation Pool has been opened to gather money in order to buy various rewards for the winning team. Depending on the pool money, the rewards can range from character fan arts to physical items.

Schedule

Registration is open from February 11th to February 24th.

(Tentative) Preliminary Phase will be held from February 25th to March 6th.

Quarterfinals will be held on March 9th at 18h00 GMT / 19h00 CET.

Semifinals and Finals will be held on March 10th at 18h00 GMT / 19h00 CET.
