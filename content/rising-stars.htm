Rising Stars

Overview

On March 1, 2019, The Rising Stars will host a tournament event that aims to highlight the new and upcoming Feast light party teams.

The Rising Stars Tournament is an intermediate level PvP team event geared towards promoting solo ranked players to step into organized team play. Teams can consist of up to 6 players. In order to keep teams within an intermediate level team must have an average rating of no more than 1700. Each team is allowed one "mentor" member whose rating will not affect your teams average rating.
